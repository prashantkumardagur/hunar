Event Progress Criteria

Participants need to register themselves using register option on the website.

Candidates whosoever have registered will then send their freshly recorded video for the audition round.

Top 100 selected participants by the judges from the audition round would compete to confirm their place for the next round and their video would be streaming live on our website.

Selected Top 50 participants would again compete to reach to the semi-finals.

Selected Top 20 semi- finalist would compete and per week 4 out of them would be eliminated and will be judged using online voting and marks given by the judges.

Top 4 finalist will then compete in the grand finale to win the Competition.
