    Event Progress Criteria

    Participants need to register themselves using register option on the website.

    Candidates whosoever have registered will then send their freshly recorded video for the audition round.

    Top 200 selected participants by the judges from the audition round would compete to confirm their place for the next round to enter the competition.

    Selected Top 100 participants would further compete to get into the Round 1 of the Mega Event. There video will be streaming live in our website.

    Selected Top 50 would compete to go to the Round 2 of the competition.

    Top 20 selected candidates will then enter to the elimination round that would be held weekly.

    After elimination Top 10 would reach in the quarter finals and compete to further confirm their place in the semi- finals.

    Top 6 selected candidates would reach the semi - finals and then compete to confirm their place in the finals.

    Top 4 selected candidate would compete in the grand finale to Win the competition.

    Perks at Season Finale

    There will be overall one winner and a runner-up.

    Winner will get a cash prize of Rs 5000/- and a recognition certificate.

    Some categories like modelling, painting, story writing will have different judgement criteria.

    Winner will get a shoutout by the celebrity.

    Participants will get the chance to interact with the celebrities.

    All the participants will be awarded with Participation Certificate.
