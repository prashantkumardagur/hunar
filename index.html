    Event Progress Criteria

    Participants need to register themselves using register option on the website.

    Top 200 selected participants by the judges from the audition round would compete to confirm their place for the next round to enter the competition.

    Selected Top 100 participants would further compete to get into the Round 1 of the Mega Event. There video will be streaming live in our website.

    Selected Top 50 would compete to go to the Round 2 of the competition.

    Top 20 selected candidates will then enter to the elimination round that would be held weekly.

    After elimination Top 10 would reach in the quarter finals and compete to further confirm their place in the semi- finals.

    Top 6 selected candidates would reach the semi - finals and then compete to confirm their place in the finals.

    Top 4 selected candidate would compete in the grand finale to Win the competition.

    Top 100 Challenge

    The top 100 challenge is a live streamed event taking place on our website where the top 100 contestants that includes every category will re-compete for a new panel of judges to score marks, rankings and detailed critiques to qualify for the further rounds.

    Top 100 challenge will live stream on 1st of September 2020.

    Perks at Season Finale

    There will be overall one winner and a runner-up.

    Winner will get a cash prize of Rs 5000/- and a recognition certificate.

    Runner up will be awarded with goodies and a certificate of recognition.

    Winner will get a shoutout by the celebrity.

    Winner of Modelling category will get a chance to work with Show director of Top beauty pageants of India.

    Some categories like modelling, painting, story writing will have different judgement criteria.

    Participants will get the chance to interact with the celebrities.

    All the participants will be awarded with Participation Certificate.

    Q.1 ) How to send the video online?

    As soon as you will register , your account will be created and you will have your own dashboard where you can upload your video.

    Q.2 ) Is constume necessary ?

    Due to this uncertain time , constume presentation will not be judged but if you have then it's fine.

    Q.3 ) Will you judge on the basis of location where it is being filmed?

    No, background of your video doesn't matter. You can shoot the video in your home, outside or where you are comfortable but in safe environment.

    Q.1 ) Why there is overall winner for different categories and why not category wise?

    We believe every category is equal and need the same efforts to perform and the category that cannot be judged with other categories will have different judgement criteria as it's already mentioned.

    Q.2 ) How will you judge a kid and a youngster as it's mentioned that there is no age category to participate?

    Talent doesn't see age, if you are capable then doesn't matter with whom you have competition and if in this little age one is able to perform in this big platform , one can imagine the experience and confidence that he/she might gain as this is not the end , it's just a start and if start is big then wonders will happen in future.

    Q.3 ) Why there is individual fee for group registration?

    Even if you are performing in group, each and every candidate of the group is important to perform, which means individual's efforts is needed and should be recognized. Kindly note that we will be providing participation certificate to each and every candidate of the group so registering individually is equally important.

    Q.4 ) Why some categories like modelling, painting etc have different winning Criteria?

    Some categories can't be compared because the way of presenting is different , for ex- Modelling is the art of presenting yourself and has different rounds in it as well and if we see painting it's an art presented in a sheet where artist put her/his thoughts, actions into a sheet so that people can visualize the same.

    Q.1 ) After performing in particular round, how sooner will I get the results?

    Soon, we will inform the result declaration date to you.

    Q.2 ) Are you offering live streaming?

    Yes, but only for candidates selected in Top 100.

    Q.3 ) Can I upload the video of the performance that I gave in any previous events?

    No, we will only accept freshly recorded video.

    Q.4 ) Do you host award ceremony online?

    Yes, we will be hosting award ceremony online and winner will get a shoutout from the celebrity.

    Q.5 ) What is Top 100 Challenge?

    Once you are selected in the audition rounds and get selected in Top 100 , Your performance will be live streamed in our website and once selected you will enter in the real competition and compete for confirming your place in quarter finals.

    Q.6 ) Who all will be the judges?

    Our judges are professional trainer in particular genre having vast knowledge and experience in every field.
